‘‘ Frequently asked questions

I've never produced a short movie before, will I be able to follow the classes?

Yes. The course has easy-to-understand language, with practical examples. The objective is to acquire autonomy to publish your audiovisual projects, either on social media or for a client.

What do I need to take the course? Equipment or software?

You just need internet access. No prior knowledge required. The course content refers to script and direction, there is no specific software content.

Can I have other activities? How long is the course?

Yes. An average of 6 hours of dynamic and recorded classes. There are no live classes. You can manage as you wish. The course was designed to be accessible, in-depth content and enjoyable to watch.

What support material is provided?

All PDF pages used in class are provided.

How long is the material available?

For two years.

Are there any group activities?

No. The course is designed for individual development.

Can I watch the units out of order?

Yes, but it is recommended that you follow the order.

Can I clarify doubts?

Yes. You will have access to the exclusive student forum.

Is there warranty?

Yes, if the course doesn´t meet your expectations, you will have 7 days and you can get your money back.

International Streaming Academy (ISA) is an online school with courses about streaming production. ISA was founded in São Paulo-SP, Brazil by university professor Larissa Perfeito who worked at the North American television network ABC and has more than 15 years of experience in teaching audiovisual production.

Mission

Offering online course with dynamism, good didactics, easy understanding and accessible. Classes with practical examples that allow learning whenever and wherever you want.

Vision

There is no geographic or technological barrier, so anyone can communicate and expose talents. The difference between who will produce streaming materials and who will not it´s knowledge and discipline.
